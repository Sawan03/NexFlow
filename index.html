FAQ

Common Questions

Straight answers to what business owners ask before getting started with Python & n8n automation.

What is the free strategy call and what do I get from it? +
A 30-minute call where we map your current workflows, identify what Python scripts or n8n flows can automate your specific tasks, and calculate time/cost savings. You receive a written audit report after the call — completely free, with no obligation to hire us.
Why Python and n8n specifically? +
Python gives us full programmatic control over complex data processing, API logic, and business rules — ideal for automation that goes beyond drag-and-drop tools. n8n is open-source, self-hostable, and extremely powerful for visual multi-step workflows, webhooks, and integrations. Together, they cover the entire range of business automation needs with production reliability.
How long does it take to deliver an automation? +
Simple automations (WhatsApp follow-ups, CRM sync, email sequences in n8n) go live within 5–7 business days. Complex Python + n8n multi-system workflows typically take 2–3 weeks. You'll receive a clear timeline before we begin — no surprises.
Do I need technical knowledge to work with you? +
None at all. You describe your business processes in plain language. We handle all the Python code, n8n configuration, and API integration. We also deliver written documentation and a walkthrough so your team understands how to monitor the automation.
What systems and tools can you integrate with? +
We integrate with any system that has a REST API or webhook. Core stack includes Python, n8n, OpenAI, WhatsApp Business API, HubSpot, Salesforce, Google Workspace, Notion, Airtable, Slack, and Zapier. If you use a tool not on this list, we can almost certainly connect it — ask during the strategy call.
What happens after go-live? +
All projects include 30 days of free post-launch support. We monitor your Python scripts and n8n workflows, fix any issues, and optimise based on real-world usage. After 30 days, you can continue with a maintenance plan or manage independently — full documentation is always provided.
How is pricing determined? +
Pricing is scoped after the free audit based on complexity and time. Simple Python scripts or n8n workflows typically start from ₹15,000. Multi-system automation projects are quoted individually with a detailed itemised breakdown — no hidden fees. You see the full scope before paying anything.
